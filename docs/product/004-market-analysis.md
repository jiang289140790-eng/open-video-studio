## Purpose

Define the market context Open Video Studio is built for.

## Business Value

Market clarity guides positioning, investment sequencing, product packaging, and long-term differentiation.

## Problem Statement

Video demand is increasing across marketing, education, commerce, entertainment, and internal communication. At the same time, production capacity remains constrained by skill, time, cost, tooling fragmentation, and review overhead. AI lowers generation cost, but the market still needs production systems that make AI output usable, repeatable, governed, and measurable.

## Scope

Open Video Studio sits at the intersection of:

- Video editing and production software.
- Generative AI media tools.
- Creative operations platforms.
- Marketing content systems.
- Collaboration and approval workflows.
- Automation and API-driven production pipelines.

Durable market drivers:

- More channels require more video variants.
- Teams need faster campaign and content iteration.
- Brand consistency becomes harder as AI output volume increases.
- Model capabilities improve faster than customer workflows can absorb.
- Businesses need governance around AI-generated media.
