## Purpose

Define the blog and educational content system for Open Video Studio.

## Requirements

- Blog content must educate, build trust, and route users toward product workflows.
- Content must avoid shallow AI hype and unsupported claims.
- Editorial categories must map to user problems and product capabilities.

## Strategy

The blog should teach users how to create better video workflows with AI: prompts, scripts, editing, repurposing, brand consistency, review, automation, and measurement.

Content categories:

- Tutorials.
- Use-case guides.
- Prompt and workflow education.
- AI video production strategy.
- Creator and marketing operations.
- Product updates.
- Customer stories.

## Conversion Path

Articles should route readers to relevant landing pages, prompt library, generation workflows, pricing, newsletter, or signup.

## Measurement

Track organic traffic, article engagement, CTA click, newsletter signup, product signup, activation, and assisted conversion.

## Governance

Content should cite product source documents and maintain editorial quality. Time-sensitive AI or competitor claims require current research.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Blog topics map to target users and product workflows.
- Articles support acquisition and activation rather than content volume alone.

## Future Plan

Add editorial calendar, content brief template, internal linking strategy, and update cadence.

## AI Context

Do not write full blog posts unless requested. Use this as the system architecture for content operations.
