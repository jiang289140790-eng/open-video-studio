## Purpose

Define email as a lifecycle, activation, retention, education, and conversion channel.

## Requirements

- Respect consent, unsubscribe, notification preferences, and privacy rules.
- Use email to help users reach product value, not spam them.
- Segment messaging by lifecycle state and user role.

## Strategy

Email should support onboarding, activation, usage education, generation tips, billing notices, product updates, reactivation, referral prompts, and customer success.

## Conversion Path

Emails route to dashboard, generate, gallery, prompt library, pricing, billing, educational content, or support.

## Measurement

Track delivery, open, click, unsubscribe, signup, activation, retained usage, paid conversion, and churn impact.

## Governance

Transactional and marketing email must be separated. Sensitive account or billing email must be accurate and auditable.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Email flows support lifecycle progress.
- Users can control communication preferences.

## Future Plan

Add lifecycle map, segmentation model, email templates, compliance requirements, and experimentation rules.

## AI Context

Email is a trust channel. Do not invent campaigns without consent, segmentation, and product-state awareness.
