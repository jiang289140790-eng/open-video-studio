## Purpose

Define long-term product sequencing for Open Video Studio.

## Business Value

Roadmap structure helps the company build compounding platform layers rather than isolated releases.

## Problem Statement

AI SaaS platforms can overbuild flashy generation before solving identity, data, workflow, permissions, reliability, or monetization. A durable roadmap must sequence foundations before scale.

## Scope

Roadmap horizons:

- Foundation: workspace model, project model, documentation, core product architecture, basic asset and video lifecycle.
- Creation: ideation, scripting, generation, editing, captions, voice, and draft creation workflows.
- Production: timeline maturity, collaboration, versioning, brand systems, review, approvals, rendering, and exports.
- Automation: reusable workflows, batch operations, integrations, APIs, webhooks, and operational pipelines.
- Intelligence: analytics, performance feedback, recommendation loops, model evaluation, and optimization.
- Enterprise: governance, auditability, compliance, procurement, advanced permissions, SSO, and administration.
- Ecosystem: marketplace, partner integrations, developer platform, and extensibility.

Sequencing principles:

- Build trust and control before high-volume automation.
- Build canonical data and permissions before collaboration scale.
- Build usage transparency before expensive AI workflows.
- Build evaluation and safety before autonomous production.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Future roadmap docs can map initiatives to these horizons.
- Teams can explain why a capability should happen now, later, or not at all.
- Roadmap decisions remain aligned with product vision and principles.
