## Purpose

Define the permanent growth source of truth for Open Video Studio.

## Requirements

- Growth work must connect to product value, not vanity metrics.
- Every channel must define audience, message, conversion path, measurement, and governance.
- Growth experiments must reference analytics and product source documents.
- This folder is documentation only and does not implement integrations, campaigns, automations, pixels, or platform APIs.

## Growth Model

Open Video Studio should grow through a combination of search intent, creator education, product-led activation, social proof, affiliate loops, referral mechanics, lifecycle email, and measurable content distribution.

Primary growth motion:

- Attract users with high-intent education and creative workflow content.
- Activate users through fast first useful video output.
- Retain users through repeatable workflows, saved prompts, assets, and production systems.
- Expand users through teams, credits, subscriptions, referrals, affiliates, and integrations.
