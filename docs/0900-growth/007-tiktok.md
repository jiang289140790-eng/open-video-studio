## Purpose

Define TikTok as a short-form education, discovery, and workflow demonstration channel.

## Requirements

- Focus on fast demonstrations, creator workflows, editing transformations, and AI video education.
- Avoid trend chasing that weakens product credibility.
- Measure downstream activation, not only views.

## Strategy

TikTok should show the speed and usefulness of AI video workflows: idea to draft, script to clip, repurposing, captions, prompt examples, and before/after edits.

## Conversion Path

Route viewers to profile link, landing pages, templates, newsletter, signup, or product examples.

## Measurement

Track views, retention, saves, shares, comments, profile visits, link clicks, signup, and activation.

## Governance

Content must not misrepresent product capabilities. Generated media examples need rights and safety review.

## Acceptance Criteria

- TikTok content teaches or demonstrates product value.
- Channel learning feeds product and content strategy.

## Future Plan

Add content series, creator collaboration policy, trend participation rules, and attribution workflow.

## AI Context

Do not produce TikTok scripts or posts here. This is the strategic operating model.
