## Purpose

Define YouTube as a long-form education, product demonstration, search, and trust-building channel.

## Requirements

- Use YouTube for tutorials, product demos, workflow explainers, comparisons, and customer stories.
- Optimize for learning and conversion, not entertainment volume alone.
- Connect videos to search, blog, landing pages, and product activation.

## Strategy

YouTube should cover deep workflows: AI video creation, prompt systems, editing workflows, social repurposing, brand consistency, and team production systems.

## Conversion Path

Descriptions, end screens, and pinned resources route to landing pages, blog posts, templates, signup, pricing, or newsletter.

## Measurement

Track impressions, CTR, watch time, retention, subscribers, link clicks, signup, activation, and assisted conversions.

## Governance

Videos must use accurate product demonstrations and approved claims. Comparisons require current research.

## Acceptance Criteria

- YouTube content builds durable trust and searchable education.
- Videos support product activation or qualified demand.

## Future Plan

Add channel programming, tutorial series, launch videos, customer story process, and production checklist.

## AI Context

Do not script or publish videos here. Use this to plan channel strategy and measurement.
