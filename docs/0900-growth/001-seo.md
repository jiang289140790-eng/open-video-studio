## Purpose

Define the organic search growth strategy for Open Video Studio.

## Requirements

- Target durable search intent related to AI video creation, editing, generation, workflow automation, and team production.
- Separate SEO strategy from implementation details such as metadata code or schema markup.
- Avoid low-quality programmatic pages that weaken trust.

## Strategy

SEO should focus on high-intent educational and solution pages that help users understand how to create, edit, repurpose, and operationalize video with AI.

Search clusters:

- AI video generator.
- AI video editor.
- Video prompt workflows.
- Social video repurposing.
- Product demo video creation.
- Marketing video automation.
- Brand-consistent video creation.
- Team video production workflow.

## Conversion Path

Search pages should route users to homepage, relevant landing pages, blog education, pricing, generation, or signup depending on intent.

## Measurement

Measure impressions, clicks, qualified organic sessions, signup rate, activation rate, retained workspace creation, and production-ready video outcomes.

## Governance

SEO content must reference Product Bible positioning and avoid exaggerated AI claims. Technical SEO implementation belongs in `docs/0800-seo/`.

## Acceptance Criteria

- SEO work maps to search intent and product value.
- Organic pages support conversion without misleading claims.
- SEO docs do not duplicate product requirements.
