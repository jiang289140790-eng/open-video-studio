## Purpose

Define the affiliate and partner growth system.

## Requirements

- Align incentives with qualified customer acquisition and retained value.
- Prevent fraud, spam, misleading claims, and payout abuse.
- Keep attribution and payout records auditable.

## Strategy

Affiliate growth should focus on creators, educators, agencies, SaaS operators, and workflow experts who can credibly teach AI video production.

## Conversion Path

Affiliate links route to landing pages, pricing, product education, or signup flows with attribution preserved.

## Measurement

Track clicks, referred signups, activation, paid conversion, retention, refunds, fraud flags, and payout eligibility.

## Governance

Affiliates must follow brand, claim, disclosure, and compliance rules. Payouts depend on validated conversion quality.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Affiliate acquisition is measurable and auditable.
- Incentives reward durable customer value.

## Future Plan

Add partner tiers, payout rules, affiliate portal, compliance review, and fraud monitoring.

## AI Context

Affiliate systems touch money and reputation. Treat attribution, disclosures, fraud, and payout integrity as core concerns.
