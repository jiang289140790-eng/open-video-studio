## Purpose

Define Pinterest as a visual discovery growth channel.

## Requirements

- Use Pinterest for evergreen visual workflows, templates, examples, and educational entry points.
- Route pins to useful landing pages, blog posts, or product examples.
- Respect rights and asset usage constraints.

## Strategy

Pinterest should focus on visual inspiration and practical workflows: video content ideas, social media video templates, AI prompt examples, product demo formats, and brand-consistent visual systems.

## Conversion Path

Pins should route to SEO pages, blog guides, templates, examples, or signup flows depending on intent.

## Measurement

Track impressions, saves, outbound clicks, landing page engagement, signup, and activated workspace outcomes.

## Governance

Visuals must follow brand guidelines and rights requirements. Avoid overpromising generated media quality.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Pinterest content is evergreen and visually useful.
- Traffic routes to relevant product or educational pages.
