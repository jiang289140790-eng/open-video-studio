## Purpose

Define the landing page system for acquisition, segmentation, and conversion.

## Requirements

- Landing pages must be tied to a clear audience, intent, and conversion path.
- Pages must not duplicate canonical product positioning from the Product Bible.
- No landing page should be created without analytics and acceptance criteria.

## Strategy

Landing pages should cover durable user segments and use cases, such as creators, marketers, agencies, ecommerce, SaaS demos, social repurposing, and team video operations.

## Conversion Path

Each page should choose one primary action: start creating, view pricing, explore examples, join waitlist, request demo, or learn workflow.

## Measurement

Track page view, scroll depth, CTA clicks, signup, pricing click, demo request, activation, and retained workspace outcomes.

## Governance

Landing pages should follow Design System rules and SEO quality standards. Claims about AI capabilities must be product-accurate.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each landing page has audience, message, CTA, analytics, and source references.
- Landing pages do not become isolated marketing experiments with no product traceability.

## Future Plan

Add landing page templates, use-case matrix, experiment process, and localization strategy.

## AI Context

Do not build or write landing pages directly from this document. Create a page spec and campaign brief first.
