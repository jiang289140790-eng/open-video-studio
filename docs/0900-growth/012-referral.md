## Purpose

Define the user referral growth loop.

## Requirements

- Referrals must reward meaningful acquisition or activation.
- Incentives must be clear, abuse-resistant, and financially sustainable.
- Referral mechanics must not compromise trust or product quality.

## Strategy

Referral should encourage users to invite creators, teammates, agencies, or collaborators who are likely to create videos and become active workspaces.

## Conversion Path

Referral links route to signup, onboarding, workspace invitation, or relevant landing pages.

## Measurement

Track referral invites, clicks, signups, activation, paid conversion, retained usage, reward cost, and abuse signals.

## Governance

Referral rewards require clear eligibility, fraud prevention, credit policy, and auditability.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Referral incentives align with retained product value.
- Abuse and duplicate attribution are considered before launch.

## Future Plan

Add referral PRD, reward policy, invitation UX, fraud rules, and credit integration.

## AI Context

Referral is a product loop, not just a share button. Design around value, incentive quality, and abuse prevention.
