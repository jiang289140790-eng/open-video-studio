## Purpose

Define the brand direction for Open Video Studio.

## Requirements

- Keep product UI calm, intelligent, professional, and creative.
- Avoid generic AI visual language and excessive ornament.
- Align copy, visual tone, and product behavior with `PB-001` and `PB-002`.

## Scope

Brand personality:

- Intelligent, not showy.
- Creative, not chaotic.
- Professional, not corporate-stiff.
- Precise, not cold.
- Calm, not passive.
- Premium, not ornamental.

Brand expression should support users doing serious creative work. The product can feel warm and imaginative, but it should never feel like a toy, generic AI demo, or template marketplace.

Voice and tone:

- Direct and helpful.
- Confident without hype.
- Human without being casual in high-stakes moments.
- Brief in operational UI.
- More expressive in onboarding, educational, and creative moments.

Visual direction:

- Minimal interface chrome.
- Strong typographic hierarchy.
- Controlled color usage.
- High-quality media presentation.
- Subtle depth and motion.
- Clear distinction between work surface, navigation, controls, and status.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Brand decisions can be explained through personality, tone, and visual direction.
- UI copy avoids hype around AI capability and focuses on user outcomes.
- Product surfaces feel premium through craft and consistency rather than decorative effects.

## Future Evolution

Future brand work may add logo usage, imagery art direction, voice examples, and marketing guidelines. Product UI should continue to prioritize clarity over brand spectacle.

## Future Plan

Add logo usage, product imagery rules, voice examples, and marketing-brand extensions when brand assets are created.

## AI Context

Use this to shape tone and visual recommendations. Do not introduce loud gradients, mascot-like language, or generic AI futurism unless a future brand decision explicitly changes direction.
