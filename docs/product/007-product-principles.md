## Purpose

Define the product principles that guide decisions across Open Video Studio.

## Business Value

Principles help teams make consistent decisions when requirements are incomplete, models change, or tradeoffs appear.

## Problem Statement

AI products can become unpredictable, gimmicky, or hard to trust. Video tools can become either too simplistic or too complex. The product needs decision rules that preserve both power and usability.

## Scope

Core principles:

- Production utility over AI novelty.
- Creative control over black-box automation.
- Workflow continuity over isolated tools.
- Trustworthy output over maximum generation volume.
- Brand consistency over one-off aesthetics.
- Explainable cost and usage over surprise consumption.
- Collaboration and review over single-player assumptions.
- Fast iteration over destructive editing.
- Canonical source documents over tribal knowledge.
- Security and rights awareness over convenience.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Feature proposals can cite the principles they support.
- Product tradeoffs can be decided without relying only on personal taste.
- AI behavior, UX, billing, and collaboration decisions remain coherent.

## Future Evolution

Principles should evolve slowly. If a principle changes, update affected roadmap, design, AI, and business model documents.

## AI Context

Use these principles when evaluating ambiguous product choices. If two principles conflict, surface the tradeoff rather than silently choosing.
