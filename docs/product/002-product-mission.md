## Purpose

Define the practical mission Open Video Studio serves for customers.

## Business Value

The mission converts the long-term vision into a concrete operating mandate for product development, positioning, onboarding, and customer success.

## Problem Statement

Video teams and creators lose time translating intent into assets, assets into edits, edits into approved deliverables, and deliverables into repeatable campaigns. AI can reduce production time, but without workflow structure it creates inconsistent output and operational noise.

## Scope

Open Video Studio exists to help users turn ideas, brand assets, prompts, scripts, footage, and workflows into high-quality videos faster, with less coordination overhead and more predictable results.

Mission statement:

Help teams create, refine, and operationalize video with AI while preserving creative judgment, brand consistency, and production control.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Product teams can use the mission to prioritize workflows over isolated novelty.
- AI features must improve production outcomes, not merely demonstrate model capability.
- Collaboration, reliability, and repeatability are treated as core product concerns.

## Future Evolution

The mission may expand from creation and editing into distribution intelligence, campaign optimization, and enterprise governance, as long as those expansions support the vision in `PB-001`.

## AI Context

When asked whether a feature belongs, test whether it helps users create, refine, or operationalize video. If it is merely interesting AI output without production utility, it likely belongs out of scope.
