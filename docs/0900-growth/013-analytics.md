## Purpose

Define the analytics framework for growth channels and experiments.

## Requirements

- Measure growth by qualified user progress, not surface engagement alone.
- Preserve attribution while respecting privacy.
- Tie channel activity to activation, retention, and revenue.

## Strategy

Growth analytics should connect acquisition source, landing intent, signup, activation, first useful output, retained usage, paid conversion, referral, and expansion.

## Conversion Path

Analytics must map each channel path from first touch to product activation and downstream value.

## Measurement

Core measurement areas:

- Channel reach.
- Qualified traffic.
- Signup conversion.
- Activation.
- First production-ready output.
- Retention.
- Paid conversion.
- Expansion.
- Referral or affiliate contribution.

## Governance

Analytics events must follow privacy, event taxonomy, and metric governance documents. Raw data access should be restricted.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each growth channel has measurable outcomes.
- Experiments define success criteria before launch.

## Future Plan

Add attribution model, dashboard specs, experiment templates, channel scorecards, and cohort reporting.

## AI Context

Do not optimize for vanity metrics. Tie growth measurement to real product value and the North Star.
