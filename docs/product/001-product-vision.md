## Purpose

Define the long-term product destination for Open Video Studio.

## Business Value

A clear vision creates strategic coherence across product, engineering, AI, design, growth, and operations. It prevents the platform from becoming a loose set of video tools and keeps investment focused on a durable category-defining product.

## Problem Statement

Professional video creation is fragmented across editors, asset tools, AI generators, stock libraries, automation systems, publishing tools, and collaboration workflows. Existing tools either optimize for manual craft, simple template generation, or isolated AI tasks. Teams need a unified AI-native production environment that preserves creative control while compressing time from idea to publishable video.

## Scope

Open Video Studio will become an AI-native video operating system for individuals and teams. It will support ideation, generation, editing, collaboration, brand consistency, asset management, workflow automation, publishing preparation, and measurable iteration.

The product should feel powerful enough for professionals, approachable enough for non-specialists, and structured enough for teams that need repeatable output.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Future product decisions can be evaluated against this vision.
- Feature proposals can explain how they advance or intentionally do not advance this vision.
- The vision supports a multi-year SaaS platform rather than a single-purpose app.

## Future Evolution

The vision may be refined as the market, models, and customer segments evolve, but it should remain anchored around AI-native video production, creative control, repeatable workflows, and professional output.
