## Purpose

Define the permanent design philosophy for Open Video Studio.

## Requirements

- Guide every future design system, page, and component decision.
- Preserve a premium AI SaaS feeling without turning product surfaces into marketing pages.
- Reference Product Bible documents for product intent.

## Scope

Open Video Studio should feel like a premium AI SaaS product: calm, precise, fast, trustworthy, and deeply capable. The product should combine the polish of Apple-like interaction quality, the operational clarity of Linear and Stripe Dashboard, the conversational intelligence of ChatGPT, the workspace familiarity of Notion, the elegance of Arc, and the deployment-grade confidence of Vercel.

This is not a decorative product. It is a professional production environment for creative and operational video work.

## Principles

- Quiet confidence over visual noise.
- Powerful workflows presented with restraint.
- AI assistance that feels explainable, reversible, and controllable.
- Dense information when users need to compare, review, and operate.
- Generous clarity when users need to decide, create, or recover.
- Premium feeling through rhythm, spacing, typography, motion, and consistency.
- Trust before magic.

## Acceptance Criteria

- New design proposals can explain how they support this philosophy.
- Interfaces avoid unnecessary ornament, novelty, and marketing-style composition inside the product.
- AI surfaces feel controllable, inspectable, and reversible.
- Product workflows remain calm under complex creative and operational states.

## Future Evolution

The philosophy should evolve slowly. Major changes require updates to design principles, brand guidelines, component standards, and accessibility expectations.

## Future Plan

Use this philosophy as the basis for future page-level design specs, editor interaction models, and product surface reviews.

## AI Context

Use this as the highest-level design lens. When choosing between visual drama and operational clarity, prefer clarity unless a product moment explicitly calls for expressive creative energy.
