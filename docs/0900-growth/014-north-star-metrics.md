## Purpose

Define how growth should align with the product North Star.

## Requirements

- Growth must optimize toward durable product value.
- Channel metrics must ladder into activation, retention, production-ready output, and revenue quality.
- Avoid optimizing solely for traffic, followers, views, or signups.

## Strategy

The primary product North Star is defined in `PB-013`: weekly approved or exported production-ready videos per active workspace.

Growth should support this by increasing the number of qualified users and teams who reach meaningful video production outcomes.

## Supporting Metrics

- Qualified visitors.
- Signup-to-activation rate.
- First generation completed.
- First production-ready video.
- Workspace retained after first output.
- Paid conversion after activation.
- Referral or affiliate quality.
- Channel-assisted production-ready videos.

## Measurement

All growth channels should report both top-of-funnel and product-value metrics. Channel success should be evaluated by contribution to activated and retained workspaces.

## Governance

North Star interpretation must remain aligned with `PB-013` and analytics definitions. Changes require updates to product and analytics documents.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Growth initiatives can explain how they contribute to the North Star.
- Teams do not confuse reach with product value.

## Future Plan

Add channel-specific North Star contribution models, dashboards, and experiment scorecards.

## AI Context

When evaluating growth ideas, ask whether they increase qualified users who create production-ready videos, not just whether they increase attention.
